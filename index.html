A Tribute Page to IPS Annamalai

Real Singham Of Karnataka

K.Annamalai was born as Annamalai Kuppusamy on monday 4 June 1984 to Kuppusamy a farmer and Parameshwari in Karur district,Tamil Nadu.He completed Bachelor of Engineering at PSG college technology in 2007 Coimbatore,Tamil Nadu. In 2010 he earned a PGDM in Business from the Indian Institute of Management in Lucknow. During his college days K.Annamalai was the secretary of samavedi society and its management circles. Further,During his Post graduation he has been as a student coordinator of the Abhiyan(Entrepreeurship of IIM in Lucknow) character and personality club

K. Annamalai began his officer training at LBSNA Mussoorie,Uttaranchal,India in september 2011 for four months.
